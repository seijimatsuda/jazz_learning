Add edge batching for iOS performance and dynamic edge threshold for visual clarity at high instrument counts.

Purpose: CANV-03 (batch rendering for iOS at high counts) and CANV-04 (weak edges auto-hide above 5 instruments). At 8 instruments there are 28 edges (C(8,2)), creating 28 ctx.save()/ctx.restore() pairs per frame.
